Our Process

How we work

Every engagement follows a structured process designed to minimize risk and maximize the chance of delivering something that actually works in production.

01
Discover
We start with a deep-dive into the business — goals, constraints, existing systems, and the specific problem we are solving. No assumptions.
02
Design
User experience, information architecture, and interface design — validated before any code is written. You see and approve the system before it is built.
03
Build
Engineering with regular check-ins, demos, and the ability to course-correct. No black box development — you have full visibility throughout.
04
Launch
Deployment, testing, and a structured handover. We do not consider the project complete until the system is live and performing as expected.
05
Support
Ongoing maintenance, feature additions, and technical support. Most clients continue working with us long after the initial build.
